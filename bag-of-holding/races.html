Dwarfs

Dwarfs are hearty creatures. They are known for being excellent craftsmen of metal and stone. While short they live to be over 400 years and tend to hold on to tradition firmly. If you don’t like to beat around the bush and would rather face a problem head on, this might be a race to consider.

Noteworthy Statistics:

  • As a Dwarf you will gain a +2 bonus to your Constitution score.
  • You will also get a +1 bonus to either your Wisdom or Strength score (It’s your choice).
  • You will be slightly slower than other, taller, races.
  • You will be able to see in the dark for 60 feet.

    • Elves

    Slender and agile, Elves are one of the most beautiful rases. They love knowledge and are more often curious than greedy. While they live to be 700 years old, Elves can hold a grudge and don’t often forget friends or enemies. If you believe the world reconvenes around you, this race could be the one for you.

    Noteworthy Statistics:

  • As an Elf you get a +2 bonus to your Dexterity score
  • You will also get a +1 bonus to either your Intelligence or Wisdom score (It’s your choice)
  • You can see in the dark for 60 feet
  • It is more difficult to surprise you and you don’t need to sleep

    Human

    This is what you are. With tons of diversity, a human can equally fulfill any roll in an adventuring party. They usually make up the majority of the world’s population in most settings. If you are looking for a Jack of all trades look no further!

    Noteworthy Statistics:

  • As a Human you will gain a +1 bonus to all of your base scores
  • You can choose another language to speak
    •

    Dragonborn

    Hatched from the eggs of dragons long ago, Dragonborn are a more human vision of their reptilian ancestors; though, they lack the wings. Many Dragonborn worship full dragons and some serve them. Many fear Dragonborn out of misunderstanding for their fierce features. If you have ever wanted to play as a dragon, this is the best bet for full filling that dream.

    Noteworthy Statistics:

  • As a Dragonborn you will gain a +2 bonus to your Strength score
  • Your Charisma score also gets a +1 bonus
  • You have a breath weapon of the same element that you descend from. You might be a Red Dragon decedent so you breath fire or a Blue Dragon and you breath lightning.
  • You are resistant to the type of damage delt by your breath weapon

    • Half-Orc

    While many find these behemoths unattractive, Half-Orcs stand as one of the tallest and strongest. They also are one of the most emotional beings. Anger burns like acid in their veins, sadness drains all of their energy and their laughter is hearty and full. If talking isn’t your thing and you’d rather brawl it out rather than argue, here’s the race for you.

    Noteworthy Statistics:

  • As a Half-Orc you will gain a +2 bonus to your Strength score
  • Your Constitution score also gets a +1 bonus
  • You can see in the dark for up to 60 feet.
  • Your best attacks do extra damage.
    •

    Tiefling

    Having the blood of an ancient and powerful evil being running through your veins has its upsides, but as a Tiefling you will always be looked down on by general society. Walking through public roads will almost undoubtedly bring stares, whispers and shouted insults. Many chose to embrace the wild blood in them but other fight back and do great good in the world. If you have ever wanted to be an outcast turned hero, now is your chance.

    Noteworthy Statistics:

  • As a Tiefling you will gain a +2 bonus to your Charisma score
  • Your Intelligence score also gets a +1 bonus
  • You can see in the dark for up to 60 feet.
  • Fire doesn’t harm you as much as it should
